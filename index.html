How to make a meme

  1. Choose a picture/gif you want to meme
  2. Decide what type of meme you want to make
    • Caption
    • GIF
    • De-motivational
    • Chart
  3. Choose wordplay to make your meme funny
  4. Do some dank editing
  5. Post your meme on Reddit or Imgflip for everyone to see!

Some famous examples

Yeet
This meme originated around 2012, and became a hit. You usually exclaim it when throwing something or a feeling of happiness
Pepe the frog
This meme originated form a cartoon an artist drew, and then skyrocketed to become one of the most popular memes of all time. It is a drawing of a very dejected looking frog. Unfortunately, it died (lost popularity) a couple years ago
Stonks Guy
This meme emerged very recently. It is one of a very poorly 3-D modeled human with terribly bad grammar. It has become very popular, and you could say it has gained a lot of 'Stonks'
